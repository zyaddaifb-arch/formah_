# Product Specification Document (Draft)

## Product Name
**Formah**

## Purpose
Formah is a smart fitness application designed to help users track their workouts efficiently, stay committed to their weekly plans, and analyze their performance over time. The app focuses on providing a structured, intelligent workout experience rather than random exercises.

## Target Users
- Fitness enthusiasts looking for structured workouts.
- Users who want to monitor progress and performance.
- Individuals seeking motivation through streaks and progress tracking.

## Key Features (MVP)

### 1. Onboarding
- 3-step introductory screens to explain app purpose and features.

### 2. Authentication Screens
- **Login**
- **Register**
- **Forget Password**

### 3. Core Screens
- **History Screen**: Displays previous workouts, progress analysis, and calendar view of workouts.
- **Start Workout Screen**: 
  - Create new workout from scratch.
  - Use pre-made workout Templates.
  - Add, edit, or delete exercises during a workout.
  - Timer support for exercises and rest periods.

### 4. Smart Streak System
- Tracks adherence to weekly workout plans.
- Encourages consistent exercise, particularly important exercises (e.g., leg day).
- Motivates users to maintain streaks without penalizing missed optional workouts.

### 5. Focus Metric
- Users can set a primary metric for each exercise (e.g., weight, reps).
- Real-time calculations and comparisons with previous workouts.
- Supports performance tracking and progress insights.

### 6. Performance Analysis
- Weekly and monthly performance tracking.
- Graphs and insights to help users understand improvement or regression.

## Design & Theme
- Dark Futuristic Fitness Theme.
- **Formah Blue Dark Theme**: dark blue background with glowing blue buttons and highlights.
- Modern, sleek UI inspired by Strong app but with a unique Formah identity.
- UX designed for simplicity, intelligence, and minimal friction.

## Notes
- Colors will be finalized by the design team.
- Focus on a seamless, motivating user experience.
- Initial version (MVP) does not include advanced social or community features.
